# ai-video-editing

**Overview**

Meishe SDK is a set of completely self-developed cross-platform video and audio processing engine, which supports mobile, web, pc. Covering the rich processing functions of the whole process, it is efficient, low consumption, and easy to expand, providing developers with competitive video and audio shooting and editing SDK capabilities.
With an extensive feature set, simple integration process, and user-friendly interface, it is a comprehensive toolkit that will bring your app to the level of industry giants. Its flexibility allows you to only pick the features that you need, optimizing the app’s download size and license costs.

**Features:**

1、AI Video Editing

Built on a proprietary non-linear editing (NLE) architecture, delivering film-grade creation capabilities.
Key Features: Supports 8K Ultra-HD, real-time processing, transitions, low resource consumption, frame-accurate editing, complex keyframe curves, multi-track timelines, and sequence nesting for deep editing and rendering.

2、AI Video Templates

Includes a vast library of templates and a dedicated production toolset.
Capabilities: Enables rapid template creation and After Effects (AE) conversion. Users can instantly generate stunning videos by applying a template and importing media. Supports secondary editing based on templates for further customization.

3、AI One-Tap Video Creation

Automated end-to-end video generation.
Workflow: Upon selecting media, the system utilizes AI content analysis to match suitable video templates, intelligently curate highlight clips, and automatically apply packaging and effects to generate the final video.

4、AI VLOG

An AI Agent for video creation powered by Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs).
Functionality: Understands user creative intent to autonomously drive material capture/screening, timeline construction, and selection of packaging effects, enabling fully personalized video creation.

5、AI Beauty & AR Effects

Comprehensive AR facial enhancement and interaction solutions.
Beauty Suite: Covers landmark-based beauty retouching, beauty templates, facial reshaping, micro-plastic surgery effects, and digital makeup.
AR Interaction: Includes 2D/3D face props, stickers, and Avatar features (face sculpting and outfit changing).

6、GAN Beauty Algorithms

Advanced beautification driven by Generative Adversarial Networks.
Technology: Trained on massive high-quality facial datasets to precisely model skin tone, texture, and facial structure across multiple dimensions.
Benefits: Achieves real-time tracking of lighting changes, ensuring consistent beauty effects across varying light conditions. Eliminates visual artifacts like flickering and jitter, enhancing immersion in live streaming and video calls.

7、AI Filters

Intelligent filter recommendation and customization.
Mechanism: Leverages on-device AI for content recognition (scene, object, color, etc.) and matching algorithms to automatically recommend the optimal filter for images or videos. Supports custom filter creation.

8、Multi-Camera Stitching & Fusion

Seamless integration of multiple camera feeds.
Algorithms: Employs distortion correction, perspective projection correction, intelligent dynamic color grading, and smart dynamic stitching.
Output: Merges footage from multiple cameras into a single cohesive view, supporting 360° panoramic surround stitching.

9、AI Highlight Subtitles

Dynamic typography for enhanced engagement.
Feature: Beyond traditional subtitles, it intelligently applies dynamic highlighting styles to spoken/sung lyrics or key content in real-time. Supports customizable highlight styles.

10、End-to-End HDR

Full-pipeline High Dynamic Range support.
Standards: Comprehensively supports HDR10, HDR10+, Dolby Vision, and HLG.
Workflow: Ensures the entire process—capture, editing, preview, and export—operates within the HDR color space with zero quality loss.

11、Memory Albums

Automated commemorative video generation.
Trigger: Based on AI detection capabilities, the system automatically triggers when album content meets specific criteria (e.g., time, location, scene).
Output: Generates memorial videos complete with timestamps, geotags, and curated copywriting/packaging.

12、Vector Search
Privacy-preserving intelligent retrieval.
Capabilities: Provides on-device text-to-image and image-to-image search capabilities, helping users quickly locate target photos in their albums while ensuring data privacy.

13、Image Quality Enhancement & Correction

Proprietary image processing algorithms for superior visual fidelity.
Features: Includes intelligent quality enhancement, video stabilization, dehazing, chromatic aberration removal (purple fringe), night scene enhancement, distortion correction, and horizon leveling. Significantly improves overall image and video quality.
